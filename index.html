About BTS

BTS (Bangtan Sonyeondan), also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band formed in 2010 and debuting in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment.

Members of the groub : Jin, Suga, J-Hope, RM, Jimin, V, and Jungkook

ARMY or A.R.M.Y (Hangul: 아미) is BTS's official fandom name. It was officially established on July 9, 2013 after the first recruitment closed, stands for "Adorable Representative M.C. for Youth"
